Humanitarian Spatial Data Center

The HSDC provides webmaps, dashboards and an extensive spatial data catalogue on disaster risk, infrastructure, demographics, terrain, administration, essential service, and much more, across Afghanistan. The platform is developed by iMMAP, funded by United States Agency for International Development (USAID) and provide to the humanitarian community in Afghanistan to enable more effective humanitarian programming and support disaster risk reduction efforts.
